Soccer Team/player tracking database

Tables

1) Teams
- team_id (PK)
- name
- city
- coach_name

Extra Constraints
- (name, city) must be unique

2) Players
- player_id (PK)
- team_id (FK -> Teams.team_id)
- name
- position
- jersey_number 
- goals 
- assists

Extra Constraints
- (team_id, jersey_number) must be unique
  - still allows same team player names & positions
